Notris is a Tetris-style falling block game made in C++ with the SDL library.

The Challenge

I decided to challenge myself to make a falling block game similar to TetrisÂ® using only C++ and SDL. I chose C++ because it is the language most commonly used in the video games industry, because I am familiar with it, and because I enjoy working with it. I chose SDL because I feel like the abstractions it provides are enough to avoid some of the fiddliness of writing cross-platform code, but still doesn't get in the way or do too much for you.

My initial goal was simple: to create a functional Tetris style game in a weekend.

The Plan

I'm a huge fan of Tetris, so I had a good idea of my requirements right from the start. I wanted more or less these features:

  • Basic Tetris functionality
    • 10x20 board
    • Movement of pieces from left to right
    • Rotation of pieces
    • Gravity to pull pieces down
    • Soft drop
    • Hard drop
  • SRS style rotation
  • Random bag based piece randomization
  • Hold Piece
  • Ghost piece
  • Scoring system
  • Level system (difficulty increase over time)
